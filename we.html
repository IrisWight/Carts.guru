Why Carts Guru?

We think that e-commerce remains above all a business

At Carts Guru, we think that e-commerce remains above all a business, in which personal contact is crucial, and in which the telephone plays an essential role.

More personal and direct than e-mail, the telephone is the most efficient means of creating a trusting relationship between the salesperson and the customer.

It enables an immediate contact to be established with prospective customers in need of advice, especially when they have just abandoned their shopping cart.

The founders of Carts Guru are experts in customer relations. In the last few years, they have met a lot of e-commerce site managers. And they are all faced with the same problem: abandoned shopping carts are responsible for up to 20% losses in turnover and they have no simple solution to this problem.

This observation led to the founding of Carts Guru, the first intelligent platform for following up abandoned shopping carts, by telephone and text messages.
